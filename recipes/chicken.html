Fried Chicken

What is fried chicken?

Fried chicken is a wonderful treat to excite the tastebuds with a combination of spice and sweet. You can add any spices you'd like, but today you'll be learning my secret Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs- Chicken cut of choice
  • 1 Cup- Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 Tbps- Black Pepper
  • 1 Tbps- Garlic or Onion Powder (Or both)
  • 1 Tbps- Coleman's Mustard Powder
  • 3 Tbps- Lemon Juice

How we gonna do this?

The directions are simple and easy to follow. In less than an hour, you'll have your new favorite snack! It's healthy too!
  1. First-

    Rub down your chicken with the lemon juice

  2. Second-

    Evenly coat all the seasons on your chicken one by one

  3. Third-

    Set airfryer to 375 and 26 minutes allow time to preheat

  4. Enjoy

Congrats on your first batch! Tweak the recipe to your liking, but remember:

You can never overseason your fried chicken!
